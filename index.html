About Me

Web Developer

Hi, I'm Tushar Chaurasiya, a dedicated and enthusiastic Data Scientist and Web Developer currently pursuing my Master's in Computer Applications (MCA). I hold a Bachelor's degree in Computer Applications (BCA) and have honed my skills through a 4-month internship in Data Analysis at Accenture.
